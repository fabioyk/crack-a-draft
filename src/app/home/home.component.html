How to upload a draft log

Where are my draft log files?

You need to opt-in to save your draft logs. By default, they are located at C:\Users\Your Windows Username\Documents. They are .txt files with your name, date and draft format.

Making MTGO save draft logs

To turn this option on, go to your Account menu

Then navigate to Game History on the left sidebar and mark the checkbox below. You can also change the destination folder here.
